Applied Computer Science

The Department of Computer is one of the main department of the college of Medicals and Applied Sciences at Charmo University. The department was founded in the academic year 2014/2015 by Computer Department at College of Education. After that, the department changed to Applied Computer as new department from new college On 2017. Only accept those students who graduated from scientific higher education. Students for professional practice and graduate study through a program that provides a strong fundamental technical and high quality undergraduate education. The vision of the Applied Computer department is to build the student's personality from several aspects in an integrated manner to be a distinct graduate of this department at all levels and has an engineer to serve the country scientific and professional ability which qualifies him for success in his career as a software.

Graduates of the department are expected to be able(outcomes)

  • A capability to apply knowledge of mathematics, science.
  • A skill to analyze and interpret data.
  • An ability to work on Multiple data (information).
  • An ability to understand, formulate, ethical responsibility during work.
  • An ability to communicate effectively through lectures.
  • A recognition of the need for, and an ability to engage in, life-long learning.
  • An ability to understand, analyze, design and implement electronic system.
  • An ability to insist to work in one or more significant application domains.

General skills for graduation at applied computer department:

  • An ability to understand the concept of Computer Basics and related tools as well.
  • An ability to arrange and design data structure, and designing and developing programs.
  • An ability to create an organize data system (data base) that supporting managers and decision makers in enterprises.
  • An ability to build computer networking with security function, and connecting with other devices.
  • An ability to understand the fundamental logic circuits.

Communication Skill during studying at applied computer department:

  • Ability to Write technical report.
  • Ability to do Oral communication and presentation.
  • Ability to communication with group and manage group as well.
  • Ability to write and present their work and their responsibility.
  • Ability to present their research.
  • Learning and practicing for group working.

Studying Language at Applied Computer Science Department Generally, the English Language is the language of education for all the subjects. Studying Duration at Applied Computer Science Department The duration of study at Applied Computer department is eight Academic semesters with full-time study. Usually an academic semester (Fall Semester) starts in September to February and Spring Semester starts February to June at one academic year.
